Korean electricity is billed on KEPCO's progressive rate system, where each kilowatt-hour gets more expensive the more you use. That makes the final amount hard to predict, and it often arrives as an unpleasant surprise at the end of the month.

This app turns an energy meter you already have on Homey into a live electricity-bill tracker. It reads your ongoing consumption and, following your meter-reading day and the seasonal progressive rates, continuously estimates what the current billing period will cost. You always know how much you have spent so far, what to expect by the end of the period, and when you are about to cross into a pricier tier, so you can adjust your usage in time and avoid bill shock.
