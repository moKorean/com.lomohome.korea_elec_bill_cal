Korea Electricity Bill Calculator

Calculate your Korean electricity bill in real time based on KEPCO's progressive rate system. Connect any energy meter device as the source and track your usage and estimated cost throughout the billing period.

REAL-TIME BILL CALCULATION
- KEPCO residential progressive rate calculation
- Low-voltage and high-voltage tariffs
- Automatic seasonal rates (Summer Jul-Aug / Winter Dec-Feb / Other)
- Billing period based on your meter reading day

DISCOUNTS
- Large family discount (5+ members, newborn household, 3+ children, life-support equipment)
- Welfare discount (national merit / disabled, social welfare facilities, basic livelihood recipients, near-poor households)

SENSORS
- This month's estimated bill and usage
- Progressive step (1-4)
- Current average tariff (won/kWh)
- Usage this hour, last hour, today and yesterday
- Daily average usage for the billing period
- Forecast usage and cost for the end of the billing period
- Comparison against the same point last month
- Last month's usage and bill
- Year-to-date usage and accumulated cost

FLOW AUTOMATION
- Trigger: progressive step changed (e.g. step 1 -> step 2)
- Condition: this month's estimated bill exceeds a given amount

SETUP
1. Add the device and select a power meter device that has the "meter_power" capability as the source.
2. In the device settings, configure your meter reading day, voltage type and any discount options.

Rates are based on the KEPCO residential tariff. Additional charges (climate/environment fee, fuel cost adjustment, 10% VAT and the 3.7% electricity industry fund) are included in the estimate.
