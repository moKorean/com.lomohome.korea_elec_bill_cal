Korea Electricity Bill Calculator

Calculate your Korean electricity bill in real time based on KEPCO's rate system. Connect any energy meter device as the source and track your usage and estimated cost throughout the billing period.

REAL-TIME BILL CALCULATION
- KEPCO residential progressive rate calculation
- Low-voltage and high-voltage tariffs
- Automatic seasonal rates (Summer Jul-Aug / Winter Dec-Feb / Other)
- Billing period based on your meter reading day

SUPPORTED CONTRACT TYPES
Besides Residential, flat (seasonal single-rate) contract types are supported. Non-residential types require a Contract Power (kW) setting.
- Residential (Low / High) - progressive
- General (A) I, Education (A), Industrial (A) I (Low / High A,B, Option I,II)
- Agricultural (A), Agricultural (B), Off-peak (A)
- General (A) II, Industrial (A) II - time-of-use (real-time off/mid/peak bucketing)
* Time-of-use types are accurate from the first full billing period after install (approximated from meter report timing).
* 300 kW+ (type B) tariffs and Off-peak (B) are not yet supported.

DISCOUNTS (Residential only)
- Large family discount (5+ members, newborn household, 3+ children, life-support equipment)
- Welfare discount (national merit / disabled, social welfare facilities, basic livelihood recipients, near-poor households)

SENSORS
- This month's cost (for usage so far) and usage
- Progressive step (1-4)
- Current average tariff (won/kWh)
- Usage this hour, last hour, today and yesterday
- Daily average usage for the billing period
- This month's forecast usage and cost (end of billing period)
- Comparison against the same point last month
- Last month's usage and bill
- Year-to-date usage and accumulated cost

EXTRA SENSORS
- Budget usage (%), current load period (off/mid/on-peak), CO2 emissions (kg), vs yesterday (%)

FLOW AUTOMATION
- Triggers: progressive step changed / step increased / new billing period / forecast exceeds budget / cost rises above amount
- Conditions: this month's cost exceeds an amount / current load period (off/mid/on-peak)
- Setting: monthly budget (won) drives the budget-usage sensor and trigger

SETUP
1. Add the device and select a power meter device that has the "meter_power" capability as the source.
2. In the device settings, configure your meter reading day, contract type (and Contract Power for non-residential types), voltage type and any discount options.
3. For accurate this-month/this-year totals, enter the meter reading at the last meter-reading day and at the start of the year in advanced settings. (These update automatically afterwards.)

Rates are based on the KEPCO tariff effective 2026-06-01. Additional charges (climate/environment fee, fuel cost adjustment, 10% VAT and the 2.7% electricity industry fund) are included in the estimate.
